Top 10 Packing Tips for a Stress-Free Move

November 04, 2025 â€¢ Swift Removals

Packing well saves time and reduces the risk of damage. These 10 packing tips will help you prepare efficiently for moving day.

1. Start early and make a plan

Begin packing non-essential items several weeks in advance. Create a room-by-room plan so you don't rush at the last minute.

2. Use quality packing materials

Invest in sturdy boxes, bubble wrap, packing tape and markers. Reusing damaged boxes increases the chance of breakage.

3. Label boxes clearly

Label each box with the room name and a short contents list. Add handling notes like FRAGILE when necessary.

4. Pack heavier items at the bottom

Place heavier items on the bottom and lighter items on top. This prevents crushing and maintains balance.

5. Protect electronics and valuables

Take photos of electronics before disconnecting them, keep original cables together, and use bubble wrap for screens.

6. Create an essentials box

Pack a clearly marked box with essentials â€” toiletries, chargers, a change of clothes, important documents and snacks.

7. Use soft items as padding

Towels, blankets and clothing can double as padding around fragile items, saving boxes and space.

8. Disassemble where possible

Remove legs from tables and beds and keep screws in labelled bags taped to the furniture.

9. Keep valuables with you

Jewellery, passports, certificates and anything irreplaceable should travel with you, not in the moving truck.

10. Check labels on arrival

When the truck arrives, place boxes directly in their destination rooms to save time unpacking later.
