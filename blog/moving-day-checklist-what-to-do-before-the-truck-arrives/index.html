Moving day brings a lot to coordinate. Use this checklist in the hours before the truck arrives to keep everything on track.

Confirm arrival time

Call your removals provider to confirm the arrival window and expected crew size.

Prepare access and parking

Reserve parking or clear the driveway. Remove obstacles that could block the loading process.

Keep pets and children safe

Arrange for pets and small children to be with a friend or in a secure room away from the moving activity.

Have tools and paperwork ready

Keep screwdriver, pliers, and any disassembly tools handy. Keep your ID, booking confirmation and contact numbers accessible.

Walk through the home with the team

Point out fragile items, special instructions and difficult access points like narrow staircases or lifts.

Do a final check

Before the truck leaves, do a quick sweep of the property for forgotten items, and lock windows and doors.
